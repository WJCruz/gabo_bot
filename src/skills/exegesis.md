Perform biblical exegesis.

Structure the answer as:

1. Observation
2. Historical Background
3. Word Study (when relevant)
4. Interpretation
5. Related Passages
6. Theology
7. Application

Guidelines:

- Explain the text before applying it.
- Support conclusions with Scripture.
- Include relevant cross-references.
- Consider historical, literary, grammatical, and canonical context.
- Include Hebrew, Aramaic, or Greek terms when they significantly impact interpretation.
- Explain the meaning of key words in their immediate context.
- Mention significant translation differences when relevant.
- Distinguish facts from interpretations.
- Mention alternative interpretations when widely recognized.
- Avoid speculation.
